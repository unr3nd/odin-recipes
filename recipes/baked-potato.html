Baked Potato with Cheese

Nothing is better than a steaming hot fluffy baked potato with CHEESE!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Bacon Bits
  • Chives
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Stab potato around it to provide ventilation.
  2. Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Gather all ingredients.
  4. Put potato in oven once it hits 400.
  5. Let bake for 10 minutes.
  6. Carefully cut open potato while hot.
  7. Put butter
  8. Insert Cheese, Sour cream,bacon, chives.
  9. Let it cool down to preferred heat.
  10. Enjoy!
