Hawaiian Pizza Recipe

Because yes, pineapple belongs on pizza.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Yeast
  • Mozzarella Cheese
  • Ham
  • Canned or fresh pineapple

Steps

  1. Prep Dough.
  2. Put Yeast under Dough.
  3. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  4. Roll out Dough.
  5. Spread tomato sauce
  6. Put cheese.
  7. Ham and any other toppings you choose.
  8. Place in oven for 15-20 mins or preferred style.
